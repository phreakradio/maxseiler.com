ABOUT

Max Seiler is a third year film student at the School of Visual Arts with a concentration in film editing. Max’s passion for film along with his professionalism and confidence has opened up opportunities for him to master many aspects of the filmmaking and showcase his own talent. His innate “visual intelligence” has been tapped for film editing and directing, cinematography, set design, storyboarding and the development of practical special effects. In High School Max was hired by the Physical Education department to film games for training purposes. He also was recruited by the school administration to produce orientation and Board of Education videos. Outside of school, Max co-founded Jaundice Boy Productions and produced several of his own films that were shown in a local theater.

Max currently resides in New York City where he attends SVA and works as a Teacher’s Assistant and at the school's equipment rental office. He is also an assistant photographer for Paws & Rewind and a technical consultant for the Shooting Star film company.
